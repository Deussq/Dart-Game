Darts Rules (301 / 501)

Starting Score: Each player begins with 301 or 501 points, depending on the chosen game mode.

Scoring: Players take turns throwing 3 darts per turn. The total points scored are subtracted from their remaining score.

Winning: The goal is to reduce your score exactly to 0. If a player scores more than the remaining points, the turn is a “bust” and the score does not change.

Finishing: The final dart must land in a double or bullseye to win the game.

Turns: Players alternate turns until someone reaches exactly 0. The first player to do so wins.
